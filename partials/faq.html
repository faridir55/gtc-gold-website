Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have full control over my funds?

Yes. Your funds remain entirely in your own brokerage account. We only have the authorization to execute trades on your behalf. We cannot withdraw or transfer your capital.

What is the minimum deposit?

The minimum required capital to copy our strategy is $1,000. This ensures sufficient margin to accommodate our automated risk management and position sizing protocols.

Are there any performance or management fees?

We charge a performance fee on profits generated, operating on a high-water mark basis. There are no upfront management fees or hidden costs. You only pay when you profit.

What are the withdrawal rules?

You can pause the copy trading and withdraw your funds at any time directly through your broker portal. There are no lock-up periods or withdrawal penalties from our end.
